## About Us

[Dentus Dentino](https://dentusdentino.md/en/) is a leading dental center in Chisinau, Moldova, offering a wide range of complex dental services and the most innovative technologies in the field. Our mission is to provide our patients with healthy and beautiful smiles.

## Our Services

We specialize in a variety of dental treatments, including:

*   **Braces and Aligners:** We offer traditional braces, as well as modern solutions like OLA and Invisalign aligners to correct teeth alignment and bite issues.
*   **Dental Implants:** We provide high-quality dental implants to restore missing teeth.
*   **Veneers and Crowns:** Our cosmetic dentistry services include veneers and dental crowns to improve the aesthetics of your smile.
*   **Pediatric Dentistry:** We offer specialized dental care for children.
